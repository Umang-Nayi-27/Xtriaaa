01 Why We Build

Privacy should not be a premium feature.

Most of the data you would rather keep private sits in three places: the posts and comments you have already made in public, the things you have quietly bookmarked, and the apps someone else could open on a shared device.

We build small, focused tools that each handle exactly one of those problems, running entirely in your browser. No dashboard to configure. No account to create before you can use it.

05 FAQ

Common questions.

Do your extensions collect or store my data?

No. Deleting, locking, and blurring happens locally in your browser. We do not run servers that see the posts, chats, or bookmarks our extensions act on.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Install the extension and it works right away. Pro upgrades go through a one-time Lemon Squeezy checkout, not a separate account system.

Which browsers are supported?

Chrome and other Chromium-based browsers, including Edge and Brave, through the Chrome Web Store.

Is there a free version?

Yes. Every extension has a free tier, with an optional Pro upgrade for extended limits and features.
